# Marketer subagent feedback — 2026-04-25

> Two rounds з симульованим CMO/Head of Marketing рівня. Збережено verbatim для historical reference + post-hackathon retro. Інсайти інтегровані у `decisions/2026-04-25-hackathon-scope-cut.md` + `brand-intel/features/*.md`.

**Persona:** Senior B2B SaaS marketer (CMO/Head of Marketing рівень) at Series A-B startup. Personally felt pain of "what does ChatGPT/Claude/Gemini say about us?" + tracked competitors manually for years.

**Subagent type:** general-purpose (anthropic-skills available, no marketing-specific). Two separate runs з різним focus.

## Round 1 — CX gaps (7 frustrations)

**Prompt focus:** Якби ти користувався BBH 3 тижні щоранку — що б тебе дратувало?

### A) Idea critique

Real felt pain = "How do LLMs describe us vs. our competitors, and is it hurting us?" Compelling. Most CMOs think вони solve це з Notion + saved Google search + 20 min/week — і mostly can.

What makes you reach for wallet = **speed of reaction**. Moment competitor launches feature → drafted response in <2h, не "let me spend tomorrow morning stitching Tavily results into Slack."

W5 simulator interesting because proactive scenario-testing ("if we reposition as X, what happens?"). Novel + defensible.

**BS items:**
- Voice morning brief = nice theater, not must-have. CMO doesn't need Telli calling at 8am — needs Slack digest або email skim while showering.
- Live widget = also theater. "What ChatGPT says about us" on website = fun but doesn't drive business.

**Real value:** W9 + W5 bundled. See competitor X launched → instantly get 3-5 counter-positioning options ranked by LLM mention rate → pick → draft live. That gap doesn't exist у Mention.com / Brand24.

### B) 7 CX gaps

1. **Approval bottleneck.** `status='draft'` — but where? No queue, no notification, no "assigned to you". W9 generates 2-3 high-severity drafts/day → pile up у SQL. Need Slack channel або email/iOS widget з "3 drafts waiting" + 1-click approve.

2. **No CMS bridge.** Approved draft → blog? X? Nowhere. Handoff = "download draft, paste into Hootsuite". Friction.

3. **Missing onboarding.** Where do I tell BBH who my competitors are? Docs say "load-competitor-list" but no UI. Manual SQL = broken product. Need `/dashboard/competitors`.

4. **No effectiveness loop.** "Did counter-draft move needle?" BBH never says "your draft last week reduced competitor mentions by 8%." No measurement = no proof of value.

5. **Team collab missing.** CMO + head of social media + agencies. No invites, roles, approvals. Single-user = year-2 blocker.

6. **Radar runs blind.** W9 surfaces only high-severity signals. No "scanned 12 sources в last 24h, found 23 signals, 2 high." Audit trail invisible. Black box.

7. **Voice = demo artifact, not feature.** Telli will fail in demo. Fallback to TTS/markdown anti-climactic. Never asked for voice. Need Slack або email digest. Priority backwards.

### C) Hackathon priority recommendation

**Ship W9 + W5 together. Skip voice + widget.**

Differentiation: pairing detection + counter-simulation = novel. Demoable: server-side, no Telli flake, no iframe CSP. Proof of value: helps CMO react faster. Low risk: Inngest functions + Tavily solid + no Firecrawl needed anyway.

4-min demo flow:
1. Open `/demo/{brand}` mobile. Show snapshot feed + narrative.
2. "Active counter-drafts" з 1 unreviewed.
3. Click "Run radar now". Inngest trace runs step-by-step. 60s: 2 new signals + 1 high-severity counter-draft auto-generated.
4. Click signal → evidence chain.
5. Click counter-draft → "Simulate alternatives".
6. W5 runs 45s. 3 ranked variants з scores. "Which one would you publish?"

Story: *automated detection → automatic draft → instant options → human decision.* Defensible.

**Risk:** No Firecrawl. If Tavily extraction brittle → pre-seed fake competitor signal у demo DB, manually trigger W9. Judges не побачать різниці.

## Round 2 — Missed use-cases (10 categories + ranked recs)

**Prompt focus:** What entire job-to-be-done categories haven't been thought through? What 2nd-order workflows would real CMO need?

### 1. Jobs beyond "competitor reacted"

W9 assumes "tell me when competitor moved → counter-punch." That's one job у suite.

- **Crisis narrative containment.** LLM surfaces "AcmeCloud data breach" або "fires 40% of team." Not competitor move — OUR move/failure. Current BBH built to react to *them*, not catch reputation forest fire about *us*. Missing: self-monitoring + triage protocol (breach ≠ pricing ≠ staffing).

- **Product launch positioning.** Shipping new feature. Need: how do LLMs describe category, where do we land, gap vs perception, which launch variant cuts through. W5 *could* test 5 launch angles — but no template. Manual. For CMO = 30% of year.

- **Pricing strategy narrative.** Raising prices → LLMs talk → competitors position against ("AcmeCloud just got 40% more expensive"). Need: simulate LLM response to pricing announcement + pre-generate counter-drafts for objections + surface competitor moves triggered by your announcement. Real-time offense + defense. Roadmap.

- **Funding announcement narrative.** Series B → press + analyst coverage + competitor FUD. LLMs synthesize. Current BBH can't shape proactively. Reactive only.

- **Sales enablement (counter-narratives → talk tracks).** W9 spits CMO-facing narrative. Sales needs talk track: "When CTO says X, you say Y because Z." Shorter, objection-handler format, deal-stage context. Missing entirely.

- **Content gap analysis.** LLMs asked "best CRM for startups" — we get 0 mentions. Why? Missing from category? Shadow-trapped by pricing? Geographically weak? Current W5 measures *our* position. Missing: "what queries are we completely absent from?" Content strategy gold.

- **Analyst report / G2 review response.** Analyst gave 4 stars, competitor 5. LLMs cite gap. Need: surface, triage, draft response (comment on G2? email analyst? change product?). Roadmap.

### 2. Persona variation (it's not all CMO)

- **In-house CMO (enterprise).** W9 daily, W5 pre-launch, approvals, team collab, cost visibility. ✓ (з CX gaps fixed)
- **VP Marketing (mid-market).** + HubSpot/Marketo integration (drafts → nurture campaigns), content calendar sync. BBH = silo right now.
- **Founder doing marketing.** Wants simplicity + ROI proof. "Did this move needle?" Current answer: ¯\_(ツ)_/¯. Will churn у 2 months. Missing: basic measurement (GA, Slack referral tracking).
- **Comms / PR Lead.** Needs (1) what *journalists* saying not just LLMs, (2) credit attribution для PR moves, (3) approval з legal. Current = pure LLM. Missing: PR signal layer (Cision/Meltwater) + legal review gate.
- **Agency (multi-brand).** New persona. 15 brands simultaneously. Current = one org_id. Need: cross-client dashboard, queueing, cost allocation, template library. Multi-tenancy. Roadmap.

### 3. Geography & language

Same brand, different market = completely different narrative. CTO у SF reads "AcmeCloud is enterprise-focused, expensive." CTO у Berlin reads (DE-localized): "AcmeCloud ist Enterprise-focused, teuer." NOT translations — perceptions differ. Pipedrive dominates EU, HubSpot dominates US.

Current BBH: English only, single-region LLM. Multi-regional CMO can't use it: (a) run W5 у 4 langs (4x cost), (b) don't test markets they're not in, (c) ship UK messaging in Japan and bomb. Missing: region-aware queries, regional LLM responses, regional competitor mapping. **New pipeline (W10 або W5 expansion).** Roadmap if chasing global SaaS.

### 4. Buyer persona mapping

W5 currently: "test variants проти 'best CRM for startups'." But *who* asks? CTO/CMO/founder/CFO — different things.

Missing: persona-aware query generation. "How would CTO phrase this? CFO?" Different LLM responses, competitive mentions. W5 should: generate persona variants, test against each, measure resonance. Achievable у W5 (add prompt step). Roadmap.

### 5. Brand persona drift

Strategy: "We are scrappy fast-deploy CRM." LLMs say: "AcmeCloud is enterprise tool with long onboarding."

Gap = strategy problem. Can't solve з counter-narratives — need to know gap exists. Missing: comparative layer. LLM perception + intended positioning = delta. High delta = (a) positioning wrong, (b) product not matching promise, (c) messaging lost. Recurring health check, not one-off. Roadmap.

### 6. Compliance & legal

Counter-draft says: "AcmeCloud has 99.99% uptime; Pipedrive doesn't disclose theirs." True? Verified Pipedrive's claims? Legal flags: FUD = lawsuit risk, lose deals на due-diligence.

Missing: (1) legal review gate у approval workflow, (2) fact-check layer (claim defensible?), (3) regulated-industry templates (fintech: required disclaimers). Non-negotiable for fintech/healthcare/insurance. Roadmap essential.

### 7. Cost discipline

Founder runs W9 + W5 a month. Bill: $1,400 у LLM tokens. No idea. Churn.

Missing: (1) tokens per run, (2) monthly cap (alert 80%, hard stop 100%), (3) cost per draft (ROI math), (4) pricing transparency (OpenAI vs Claude per use-case).

**Hackathon: cost badge per run output ("This run cost $8.50"). 2h work, massive credibility.** Roadmap: budget gates + cost attribution per brand (agencies).

### 8. Integration ecosystem

CMO stack: HubSpot + Slack + Notion + Linear + Google Docs. BBH right now = separate dashboard, separate queue, output = text. Workflow: W9 surfaces → I draft → I want HubSpot nurture → Slack notify sales. Current = copy-paste.

Non-negotiable post-hackathon:
1. **Slack notification** (signal → alert, draft → alert, approval → thread).
2. **HubSpot integration** (draft → nurture campaign або email sequence).
3. **Google Docs / Notion** (export для editing або embed у war-room).

Missing = BBH = tool you check, не tool у workflow. Hackathon: maybe Slack notification.

### 9. Efficacy proof

"Did your counter-narrative work?" BBH answer: "I don't know. I'm not connected to email/CRM/revenue."

Real measurement:
- **Share-of-voice delta:** "Before narrative, LLM mentioned us 12%. After 30 days, 18%." Measurable (weekly LLM snapshots, mention rate).
- **Citation rank:** "Moved from position 4 to 2 in LLM responses about 'CRM for enterprise.'" Proxy for mindshare.
- **Deal velocity:** "Deals using your draft close 8% faster." Requires HubSpot CRM integration + tagging.

Hackathon: show *hypothetical* framework ("here's what we'd track if HubSpot connected"). Roadmap: build measurement layer.

### 10. Risk / failure modes

**Scenario 1: Hallucinated counter-narratives.** LLM invents fact. Sales quotes it, customer calls BS, deal dies. Mitigation: fact-check layer.

**Scenario 2: Auto-generated feel.** "Reads like AI." Approval team rejects, treats BBH as busywork. Mitigation: brand-voice tuning + human-in-loop edit (not just approve/reject).

**Scenario 3: Over-reaction to noise.** Medium signal classified HIGH → drafts → approval annoyed → turn off W9. Mitigation: classifier tuning (mark false positives, retrain) + per-brand thresholds.

**Scenario 4: Offline success.** Draft perfect, deployed, LLMs don't update. Why? Training lag (6mo). Or competitor moved more. Or positioning не resonates. Mitigation: set expectations (LLM lag = weeks/months), 90-day rolling window, no month-1 promises.

## Top 3 to ADD to demo today (even at risk of overscope)

1. **Cost badge.** "This radar run analyzed 150 signals, cost $2.80." Transparency = trust. 2h work.
2. **Slack notification.** New HIGH signal або simulator result → Slack channel. Removes "remember to check dashboard" friction. 3h work.
3. **Persona-aware W5 variant.** Test positioning проти "CTO at 50-person startup" + "CFO at 50-person startup." Buyer nuance. 4h work, can be hard-coded prototype.

## Top 3 on roadmap (never compromise)

1. **Measurement layer.** Weekly LLM snapshots + share-of-voice + citation rank. Without це BBH = cost center, не revenue lever. Q2.
2. **Slack + HubSpot integration.** Notifications + campaign auto-creation. Tool у workflow vs tool you check. Q2.
3. **Legal review gate + fact-check layer.** Regulated industries non-negotiable. Q3.

## Top 3 risks pre-launch

1. **Classifier brittleness.** False positives/negatives. Solution: seed initial training з real competitor signals + customer triage. First 3 customers hand-label 100 signals.
2. **Over-promise on LLM speed.** "Deploy today, LLMs cite tomorrow." Reality: weeks. Set expectations у onboarding, measure 90 days.
3. **Brand voice misalignment.** "Synergistic, cutting-edge" when brand says "boring, reliable, scrappy." Pre-launch: fine-tune LLM з brand voice (Slack archives, past marketing). Measure: <20% draft rejection rate.

**Bottom line:** Built tool для CMOs to monitor + react. Haven't built tool to measure / integrate / operate at scale. Difference between "cool prototype" і "we pay every month."
